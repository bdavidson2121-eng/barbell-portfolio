Decay Math — Why 3x Inverse ETFs Erode

A sequence of +10% then −10% on the index:

Index: $100 → +10% → $110 → −10% → $99 (−1%)
3x Bull ETF: $100 → +30% → $130 → −30% → $91 (−9%)
3x Bear ETF: $100 → −30% → $70 → +30% → $91 (−9%)
Formula: (1 + 3r)(1 − 3r) = 1 − 9r² — volatility drag is 9× the index variance
